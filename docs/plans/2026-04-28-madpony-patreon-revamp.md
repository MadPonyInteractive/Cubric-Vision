## 5. Tier Ladder

> **Model note:** Cubric Studio core is open source (AGPLv3), free for everyone. Patreon funds development and unlocks the layer on top: agents, skills, early builds, and direct access. Public gets portable builds ~1 month after Pro.

### Tier 1 — Supporter — £1/month

Want to keep an independent open source project alive? This tier is for people who've used a video, a tool, or a tutorial and want to say thanks. Every pound funds the next one.

**You get:**
- **Supporter** Discord role with custom colour and badge
- Your name credited in Cubric Studio's About panel and all free tools
- Behind-the-scenes dev posts before they hit YouTube

*Best for: viewers and casual users who want to support the work.*

---

### Tier 2 — Early Access — £5/month

Active-creator tier. Get every Cubric Studio portable build **two weeks before** public release, plus tutorial project files and the Discord channels where real conversations happen.

**You get:**
- **2-week early access** to every Cubric Studio portable release (Windows, Mac, Linux)
- **Tutorial project files** — ComfyUI workflows, source files, prompt packs from every new tutorial
- **Early Access** Discord role + private channels for build discussion, workflow sharing, and feedback
- Vote on upcoming features and tutorials
- Everything in Supporter

*Best for: AI creators, ComfyUI users, and anyone running Cubric Studio in real work.*

---

### Tier 3 — Pro — £15/month

Bleeding-edge tier. Day-1 builds. Exclusive project templates. Direct line to me. Every paid Mad Pony tool included free.

**You get:**
- **Day-1 access** to every Cubric Studio portable build — compiled and in your hands immediately (two weeks ahead of Early Access, one month ahead of public GitHub release)
- **Pro Project Templates** — drag-and-drop onto the app, everything pre-configured and ready to generate. Built to pair with tutorials. Templates are Pro-exclusive; no equivalent at lower tiers
- **All paid tools free** — every paid Mad Pony tool (Gumroad / ArtStation / FlippedNormals) included while your subscription is active
- **Commercial-use license** for Cubric Studio output and all included tools
- Your name **prominently credited** in Cubric Studio's About panel as a Pro sponsor (limited slots — your name actually stands out)
- **Pro** Discord role + 1-on-1 feedback channel with direct responses from me
- Priority on bug reports and feature requests
- Everything in Early Access

*Best for: power users, freelancers, and small studios who depend on these tools professionally.*
