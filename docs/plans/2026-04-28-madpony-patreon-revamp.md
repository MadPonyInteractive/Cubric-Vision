I'm Fabio — a solo developer building tools for creators.

For years that meant ZBrush macros, Blender add-ons and tutorials helping artists work faster. Today, that same mission has shifted into AI. Local AI specifically — running powerful open-source models on your own machine, no cloud fees, no censorship, no data going anywhere it shouldn't.

I'm now building **Cubric Studio**, a frictionless, 1-click local AI desktop app powered by ComfyUI. It lets anyone generate images, video, audio and text locally without wrestling with dependencies or spaghetti-node graphs. This Patreon is the home for everything I make under Mad Pony — Cubric Studio, future creator tools, and the tutorials that go with them. Tutorials going forward focus on Cubric Studio, ComfyUI, and AI-native dev workflows (agentic IDEs, local model setups, production pipelines).

By backing this Patreon, you're funding independent tool development — and getting early access, project files, and direct support in return.

Old ZBrush and Blender tutorials on YouTube stay up. New ones won't be coming. The pivot is to AI, and you're getting in on the ground floor.

Let's build the tools the AI creator economy actually needs.

## 5. Tier Ladder

### Tier 1 — Supporter — £1/month

Want to say thanks for a video, an app, or a free tool that saved you time? This tier is for casual fans who want to keep this independent project alive without thinking about it. Every pound helps fund the next tool.

**You get:**
- **Supporter** Discord role with custom colour and badge
- Your name credited in the "About" section of all free tools
- Read behind-the-scenes development posts before they hit YouTube

*Best for: viewers who want to support the work without committing to active use.*

---

### Tier 2 — Early Access — £5/month

The active-creator tier. Get every Cubric Studio build **two weeks before** the public release, plus all tutorial project files and the curated Discord channels where the actual conversations happen.

**You get:**
- **2-week early access** to every Cubric Studio release
- **Tutorial project files** — ComfyUI workflows, source files, prompt packs from every new tutorial
- **Early Access** Discord role + access to private channels for build discussion, workflow sharing, and feedback
- Vote on upcoming features and tutorials
- Everything in Supporter

*Best for: AI creators, ComfyUI users, and anyone using Cubric Studio in real work.*

---

### Tier 3 — Pro — £15/month

The bleeding-edge tier. Day-1 access to everything. Direct line to me. Plus every paid tool I make is yours free for as long as you're active.

**You get:**
- **Day-1 access** to every Cubric Studio build — the second it's compiled, you get the link (a full month ahead of public, two weeks ahead of Early Access)
- **All paid tools free** — every paid Mad Pony tool (Gumroad / ArtStation / FlippedNormals) is included free while your subscription is active. Cancel any time.
- **Commercial-use license** for Cubric Studio output and any included tools
- Your name **prominently credited** in Cubric Studio's About panel as a Pro sponsor (limited count = your name actually stands out)
- **Pro** Discord role + access to a 1-on-1 feedback channel where you can post work and get direct responses from me
- Priority on bug reports and feature requests
- Everything in Early Access

*Best for: power users, freelancers, and small studios who depend on these tools.*
