# Code Of Conduct

Cubric Vision uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a healthy project:

- being respectful of different experience levels and backgrounds;
- giving specific, actionable feedback;
- keeping technical disagreement focused on the work;
- accepting maintainer decisions once a direction is chosen.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks;
- public or private abuse of contributors or maintainers;
- publishing private information without permission;
- sustained disruption of issues, Pull Requests, or community spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or Pull Requests, restrict
participation, or ban contributors whose behavior is harmful to the project.

Report conduct concerns through the same private channel used for security
reports when possible. If no private channel is available yet, open a minimal
public issue asking for maintainer contact without posting sensitive details.
